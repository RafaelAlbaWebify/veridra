# Veridra 3.0.0

Veridra 3.0.0 adds a local commercial-operations workflow around captured audit leads.

## Added

- Lead ownership, next action, last-contacted and next-follow-up fields.
- Owner, status and overdue-follow-up visibility.
- Deterministic tracked report and CTA links.
- Privacy-bounded report-open and CTA-click event persistence.
- Commercial summaries for lead status, owner, follow-ups, engagement and delivery outcomes.
- Engagement CSV export.
- Retention preview and explicit prune operations for old terminal leads and engagement events.
- Operator routes for commercial overview and lead operations.

## Privacy and evidence boundary

Engagement tracking uses no cookies, pixels, fingerprinting or third-party analytics. It stores only the related local identifiers, event type, timestamp and bounded request-header evidence.

## Retention boundary

Retention never implicitly deletes assessments, projects or remediation tasks. Only old terminal leads and old engagement events selected by an explicit preview can be pruned.

## Commercial limits

This release does not infer revenue, attribution, marketing ROI or unique people. It does not provide authentication, account tenancy, public SaaS deployment, background jobs or billing.
