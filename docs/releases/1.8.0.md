# Veridra 1.8.0

- Adds twelve deterministic Local Presence findings from already collected website evidence.
- Detects LocalBusiness and relevant subtype JSON-LD, including nested graphs and type arrays.
- Checks structured name, URL, telephone, postal address, opening hours, and sameAs references independently.
- Checks visible telephone, address, opening hours, map or directions, and location-route signals independently.
- Records detected structured-data types, invalid JSON-LD blocks, and bounded visible-signal evidence.
- Adds Local Presence findings to reports, evidence exports, and the no-registration free tool.
- Does not query Google Business Profile, Maps rankings, reviews, citations, or external NAP consistency.
