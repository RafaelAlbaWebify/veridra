# Veridra 2.8.0

Veridra 2.8.0 expands reusable white-label profiles into richer agency report templates.

## Added

- Selectable assessment areas with summaries recalculated from included findings only.
- Deterministic section selection and ordering.
- Optional cover title, executive summary, conclusion and agency contact details.
- Bounded embedded PNG/JPEG logo data with no remote asset fetching.
- Transparent business-impact grouping based on observed finding areas, status and severity.
- Implementation-roadmap grouping without synthetic scores, financial estimates or automatic ownership claims.
- Priority actions ordered by explicit severity and deterministic tie-breakers.
- Consistent profile behavior across HTML reports, server-generated PDFs and evidence ZIP reports.
- Expanded local profile creation, editing and review controls.

## Safety and scope

- Embedded logos are limited to validated PNG or JPEG data URIs of at most 200 KB.
- Remote logos, scripts, fonts and stylesheets are not fetched during report rendering.
- Business-impact and roadmap sections are presentation layers over existing evidence; they do not estimate revenue loss, legal exposure or remediation cost.
- This release does not claim PDF/A, tagged-PDF accessibility, editable PDF output, custom-domain hosting, analytics or cloud template sharing.
