# Veridra 1.7.0

- Adds a separate public-style free-tool surface at `/free`.
- Provides focused Website Audit, AI Readiness, Trust Signals, Security Posture, and Local Presence Readiness entry points.
- Runs existing bounded public assessments without registration or anonymous persistence.
- Shows useful scoped findings and deterministic query-state sharing before any account workflow.
- Keeps local projects, report profiles, and assessment history out of the anonymous interface.
- Labels Google Business Profile, Maps, review, citation, AI-presence, and penetration-testing limitations explicitly.
- Extends Chromium desktop/mobile and keyboard validation to the free-tool surface.
