# Veridra 2.2.0

Veridra 2.2.0 deepens the bounded commercial crawl with sitemap-assisted discovery and evidence-backed broken internal-link findings.

## Added

- Same-origin sitemap discovery from robots.txt and `/sitemap.xml`.
- Bounded sitemap-index and URL-set parsing.
- Deterministic deduplication of sitemap and HTML-link targets.
- Internal-link source-page tracking.
- Broken-link findings for failed collection and HTTP 4xx/5xx targets.
- Sitemap failure evidence without assessment crashes.

## Boundaries

Crawling remains sequential and subject to page, depth, response-size, total-byte, redirect, timeout and public-target controls. External origins, unsupported schemes, fragments and successful non-HTML resources are not classified as broken internal links. Veridra does not execute JavaScript, authenticate, submit forms or perform active security scanning.
